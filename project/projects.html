Our Initiatives

Driving change through dedicated action. Explore our core projects.

Project Seva

Serving Humanity with Compassion

Project Seva is the heart of InAmigos Foundation. We believe that no individual should go to sleep hungry or lack basic clothing. Through Project Seva, our dedicated volunteers organize massive food distribution drives in underserved areas and slums.

In addition to food, we conduct seasonal donation drives to provide warm clothing, blankets in the winter, and essential hygiene kits to those living in extreme poverty. Our goal is to preserve human dignity and provide immediate relief to vulnerable populations.

Support Seva

Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing Young Minds

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. Under Project Bachpanshala, we set up community learning centers and tutoring camps for children who cannot afford formal schooling or who need extra academic support.

We provide free stationery, books, and regular mentorship to ensure these children stay in school and dream big. By focusing on holistic development, including arts, sports, and moral education, we aim to build a strong foundation for their bright futures.

Support Bachpanshala

Project Jeev

Compassion for All Living Beings

Animals share this planet with us, yet they are often the most neglected. Project Jeev is our dedicated animal welfare initiative. We run daily feeding programs for stray dogs, cats, and cows in various localities.

Beyond feeding, we actively participate in rescue operations for injured animals, arrange necessary medical treatments, and conduct awareness campaigns to promote empathy and prevent animal cruelty in communities.

Support Jeev

Project Udaan

Soaring Towards Independence

Project Udaan focuses on empowering women from marginalized communities by providing them with the tools they need to achieve financial independence and self-reliance.

We organize vocational training workshops ranging from tailoring and handicrafts to digital literacy and basic computer skills. Additionally, we conduct awareness sessions on women's rights, health, and hygiene, ensuring they have the confidence to step up and lead.

Support Udaan

Project Prakriti

Healing Our Planet

Environmental conservation is no longer an option; it's a necessity. Project Prakriti is our pledge to Mother Earth. We regularly organize large-scale tree plantation drives and take responsibility for their long-term survival.

Our volunteers actively participate in cleanliness drives across public spaces, riversides, and parks. We also run campaigns to reduce single-use plastic and educate the youth on sustainable living practices.

Support Prakriti

Project Vikas

Empowering the Youth

The youth are the architects of our future. Project Vikas is aimed at bridging the gap between education and employability. We offer comprehensive internship programs that provide practical, hands-on experience in various domains.

Through skill-building workshops, resume writing sessions, and career counseling, we have successfully onboarded over 1 Lakh+ interns, making us a top employer partner. We ensure that young minds are equipped to thrive in competitive professional environments.

Support Vikas

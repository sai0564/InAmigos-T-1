Project Seva

Serving Humanity with Compassion

Project Seva is the heartbeat of InAmigos Foundation. We believe no individual should go to sleep hungry or lack basic clothing. Through Seva, our dedicated volunteers organize large-scale food distribution drives in underserved communities, slums, and shelter homes.

We also conduct seasonal donation drives to provide warm clothing, blankets during winter, and essential hygiene kits to those living in extreme poverty. Our mission is to preserve human dignity and provide immediate relief to the most vulnerable.

Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing Young Minds

Education is the most powerful weapon to change the world. Under Bachpanshala, we set up free community learning centers and tutoring camps for children who cannot afford formal schooling or need extra academic support.

We provide free stationery, books, and regular mentorship. By focusing on holistic development — arts, sports, and moral education — we build a strong foundation for every child's bright future.

Project Jeev

Compassion for All Living Beings

Animals share this planet with us, yet they are often the most neglected. Project Jeev is our dedicated animal welfare initiative. We run daily feeding programs for stray dogs, cats, and cows in various localities across Chhattisgarh.

Beyond feeding, we participate in rescue operations for injured animals, arrange necessary medical treatments, and conduct awareness campaigns to promote empathy and prevent cruelty in communities.

Daily Feeding Rescue Operations Medical Aid 500+ Animals

Project Udaan

Soaring Towards Independence

Project Udaan focuses on empowering women from marginalized communities by providing them with the tools they need to achieve financial independence and self-reliance.

We organize vocational training workshops — from tailoring and handicrafts to digital literacy and basic computer skills. We also conduct awareness sessions on women's rights, health, and hygiene, ensuring every woman has the confidence to lead.

Project Prakriti

Healing Our Planet

Environmental conservation is no longer an option — it's a necessity. Project Prakriti is our pledge to Mother Earth. We regularly organize large-scale tree plantation drives and take responsibility for their long-term survival.

Our volunteers actively participate in cleanliness drives across public spaces, riversides, and parks. We also run campaigns to reduce single-use plastic and educate youth on sustainable living practices.

Project Vikas

Empowering the Youth

The youth are the architects of our future. Project Vikas bridges the gap between education and employability. We offer comprehensive internship programs providing practical, hands-on experience in various domains.

Through skill-building workshops, resume writing, interview preparation, and career counseling, we have successfully onboarded over 1 Lakh+ interns — making us a top employer partner on Internshala for 5+ consecutive years.

Get to Know Us

India's Heart-Driven NGO

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). Registered as a Section 8 non-profit under the Companies Act 2013, we are licensed by the Central Government and headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

With a powerful network of dedicated volunteers, corporate partners, and passionate changemakers, we work tirelessly to uplift communities across India through six flagship initiatives.

Why We Exist

The Purpose Behind Our Work

Each project tackles a real, urgent social need — here's the impact we are creating together.

No One Sleeps Hungry

Project Seva ensures thousands of meals are distributed weekly, preserving the dignity of every individual regardless of their circumstances.

Education as a Right

Bachpanshala creates safe learning spaces for children who lack access to formal schooling, breaking the cycle of poverty through knowledge.

Compassion Without Boundaries

Project Jeev advocates for the voiceless — rescuing, feeding, and providing medical care to animals abandoned on India's streets.

Women Leading Change

Udaan transforms lives by equipping women with skills that open doors to financial independence, self-confidence, and leadership.

A Greener Tomorrow

Prakriti combats climate change at the grassroots — planting trees, cleaning communities, and educating citizens on sustainable living.

Youth Ready for the World

Vikas bridges the gap between college and career, helping India's youth develop professional skills and land meaningful opportunities.
